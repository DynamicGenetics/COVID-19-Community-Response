Identifying vulnerability


This map is an opportunity to explore data from government, community and social media sources to better understand the distribution of community support and community-level vulnerabilities in Wales during the COVID-19 pandemic. Citizen-led support describes the support provided by self-organising communities and third sector organisations. You can choose different variables to explore and answer the questions that you are most interested in.

Community support


Since the pandemic started communities have been mobilising to help each other; from shopping for elderly neighbours, to offering to sew face masks. Understanding which communities might have lower levels of community cohesion and organisation, as well as vulnerabilities, can help government agencies and third sector organisations consider which areas might need some extra help from extra voluneers or community support groups.
