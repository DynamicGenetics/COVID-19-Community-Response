Identifying vulnerability


This dashboard is an opportunity to explore data from government, community and social media sources to better understand the distribution of community support and community-level vulnerabilities in Wales during the COVID-19 pandemic. You can choose the variables you want to compare, to answer the questions that you are interested in.

Community support


We want to understand which areas have more vulnerability to COVID-19 and less community support. These areas may need more help from extra volunteers or community support groups. Since the pandemic started communities have been mobilising to help each other; from shopping for elderly neighbours, to offering to sew face masks. Different places might have more supportive communities, and features of communities that might mean COVID cases are more likely. Understanding which communities might have lower levels of community support, and more vulnerabilities, can help government agencies and third sector organisations consider which areas might need some extra help.
